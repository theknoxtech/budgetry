Executive Summary

The personal finance app market is saturated, yet user dissatisfaction remains high. Our research indicates a massive opportunity lies at the intersection of Automated Intelligence and Reliable Infrastructure. While users crave AI-driven insights, they are leaving apps due to fundamental flaws like broken bank connections and manual labor.
